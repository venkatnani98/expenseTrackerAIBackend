03 — How We Use It

Why we use your data

Your data is used exclusively to provide, maintain, and improve Expense Tracker. Specifically:

  • To authenticate you and secure your account
  • To store, display, and analyse your expense records
  • To generate spending reports, charts, and budget summaries
  • To send transactional emails (password reset, export confirmations)
  • To detect fraud, abuse, and security incidents
  • To improve app performance and fix bugs

We will never use your data for targeted advertising or sell it to third parties.

04 — Data Sharing

Who we share data with

We share personal data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — cloud hosting, email delivery, and analytics tools under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal obligations — when required by law, court order, or to protect rights and safety
  • Business transfers — in the event of a merger or acquisition, you will be notified before your data is transferred
We never sell, rent, or trade your personal information or expense data to any third party for commercial purposes.
05 — Storage & Security

How we protect your data

Your data is stored on secure servers with the following safeguards in place:

  • All data in transit is encrypted via TLS 1.2+
  • Passwords are hashed with bcrypt (never stored in plain text)
  • Database backups are encrypted at rest
  • Access to production systems is limited and logged
  • Regular security audits and dependency updates

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all personal data is permanently removed within 30 days.
